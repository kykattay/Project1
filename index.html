Check out our special new single-origin Colombian coffee. Each cup of this is smooth and balanced, making it the best way to start your morning.

origin


These beans are from the Valle del Cauca region of Colombia. This region’s land has characteristics like water basins, good temperatures, and soils with volcanic ashes in it.


experience


These beans are best when used for Chemex, Pour Over, Siphon, Drip, and French Press


notes


Dark Chocolate, Almond

The notes of dark chocolate and almond give the beans a rich and nutty flavor. The roasting of the beans bring the flavors to life when brewed.

ABOUT THE CREATORS


Canyon Coffee was started out of the apartment of the founders, Ally and Casey, in 2016. Their relationship started to revolve around coffee and they started to explore and learn more. They became motivated to turn a passion of theirs into a business. They began selling their coffee online and then started doing pop-up shops on the weekends. Over a year later they ventured into selling their coffee to other businesses. Now, many years in, their coffee is all throughout businesses in the Los Angeles area and is to continue to grow to farther parts of the country.
